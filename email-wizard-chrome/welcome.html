Welcome to Email Wizard!

Step 1: Connect to Gmail

Click the Email Wizard icon in your browser toolbar and press the "Connect to Gmail" button.

Step 2: Grant Permissions

You'll be asked to grant Email Wizard permission to access your Gmail account. This is necessary for the extension to work.

Step 3: Start Using Email Wizard

Once connected, you'll see a "Summarize" button next to each email in your Gmail inbox. Click it to get an AI-generated summary of the email.

Enjoy using Email Wizard to boost your email productivity!
